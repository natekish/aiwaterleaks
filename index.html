Data Disclaimer & Contributions

Data Transparency Notice: The information presented in this application is compiled from verified Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests submitted to public utilities, direct water usage reports from data center operators, and watershed replenishment data sourced from verified corporate sustainability reports. While every effort is made to ensure data integrity, all information is provided "as is" for transparency and public oversight purposes.
