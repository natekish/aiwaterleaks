Commit to Water Transparency

Help us build a more sustainable future by contributing data.

👥 For the Public

  • Request data from local municipalities using FOIA.
  • Upload utility bills or PDF reports found online.
  • Help identify unlisted data center locations.

🏢 For Operators

  • Voluntarily disclose annual water consumption.
  • Share project details for restoration offsets.
  • Provide direct CSV/Excel data for higher accuracy.

How to make a FOIA Request

1. Identify Agency: Contact the city water department or utility provider for the site.
2. Use Template: Download FOIA Template. Specify dates (e.g. 2022-2024).
3. Submit & Track: Submit via email. Agencies must usually respond within 5-10 days.
