Data Disclaimer & Contributions

The data presented in this application was obtained through formal Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests submitted to the respective public utilities. While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, this data is provided "as is." The authors do not guarantee that the data is free from errors or processing delays by the original sources.

Contribute Data: Please submit any verified water usage records to dcwaterleaks@gmail.com.
