Data Disclaimer & Contributions

The data presented in this application was obtained through formal Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests submitted to the respective public utilities. While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, this data is provided "as is."

Contribute Data: We encourage the submission of verified water usage records by the public, community groups and especially by data center operators to datacenterwaterleaks@gmail.com.

File Your Own Request: Download our FOIA Template (Word Document).
